Trading Space

prop firm journal

Small project · Free to use
What is this?

Trading Space is a small personal project built for prop firm traders who want a clean, distraction-free way to log their trades and track their performance over time.

It started as a simple idea — a journal that actually understands the prop firm world, with drawdown tracking, profit targets, multiple accounts, and strategy analysis built in from the start.

What's included
📋
Trade Journal
Log every trade with full detail — entry, SL, TP, risk %, R:R, screenshots and notes.
📈
Performance Analytics
Breakdown by week, month, asset, session and strategy with win rate and PnL stats.
⊞
Multi-Account
Track multiple prop firm accounts with individual targets and drawdown limits.
⚡
Strategy Builder
Define your strategies with entry/exit rules, pairs and sessions. See their live stats.
📅
Calendar View
Monthly PnL heatmap so you can see your best and worst trading days at a glance.
✎
Daily Journal
Write daily notes with mood tracking, market analysis and plan for tomorrow.
